## B. Master’s Programmes (evaluate in Year 2–4)

Pursue a master’s **after** first registration/employment unless a scholarship makes earlier study strategic.

| Programme type | Example directions | Best if targeting |
|---|---|---|
| MSc Neurological Rehabilitation / Neurophysiotherapy | Stroke, neuroplasticity, advanced neuro practice | Neuro centres, UK/AU hospitals |
| MSc Musculoskeletal / Sports & Exercise Physiotherapy | Advanced MSK, sports clinics | Private MSK / sports |
| MSc Rehabilitation Science / Health Science | Research + academic bridge | University hospitals, PhD later |
| MPH (selective) | Systems, community rehab, policy | Community / leadership track |
| German-taught or English M.Sc. in Europe | Mobility + recognition adjacency | Germany/EU long game |

**ROI rule:** Do not spend on a master’s to “fix” a weak registration dossier. Fix English + credentialling first.

**Funding watch:** Chevening (UK), Australia Awards (limited), DAAD (Germany), institutional scholarships, employer sponsorship after Year 2.
